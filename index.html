"The opposite of love is not hate: it is indifference"


A short and growing foxkin with a creamy blonde head of hair, which melts ever so gently atop his sun kissed face. Steel eyes, as sharp as their name sake, peer over a face inspired by either the pouting moon or the rising sun. Two small soft ears jut from where they normally sit, as fuzzy as seeding pods. His dexterous body moves without hurry despite great potential and his eyes often wander, but to the unusually asute who may draw notice: his attention never slips.

Once orphan and refugee, forged in the calamity of the recent past, Casildo yet hosts an ember of the fires that bore him. It flickers and glints in his eyes, as if flames themselves could be pointed and sharp. His sly and unrepentant smile bathes those who bear it with a warm and open dare as they proclaim an open challenge: "I see you. Show me more." Two sharp canines arm his expression.

For in his heart burns not the malice and rage of war and conquest, of spilled blood yearning for companionship, but of the stars, meadows, and the colors to be found waving in the eyes of the other. His ears rise to music, his fingers draw shapes to stand, and a new face never fails to draw his eye.
